About

Team Sky Nite began as a Diablo 2 clan before venturing into MMORPGS such as Maplestory and Phantasy Star Online. First known as Superior Chaos Pirates, Team Sky Nite began their venture into esports with the release of Starcraft 2 in 2010 where shortly after Team Sky Nite was formed.

Whether you're a casual-gamer looking to hang out on the weekends or some aspiring pro-gamer looking to win, join our forums for our like-minded gamers and check out the forums for all your gaming needs.
